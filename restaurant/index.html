Welcome to DeLaney's Deli and Rotisserie! We appreciate your interest and are excited to share our allergen-free menu with you. Allergies have become more common than ever in America today, but that doesn't mean you can't enjoy eating out with family and friends. Our menu accommodates wheat-free, dairy-free, and egg-free dining.

We come from a large family with a variety of allergies. This led our wonderful mom to develop her own flour blends. Mama's recipes are the cornerstone of our restaurant. We learned to appreciate natural foods at a young age and look forward to sharing these delicious options with you.
